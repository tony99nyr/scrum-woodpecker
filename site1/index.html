Did you know the Red-headed Woodpecker is one of only four North American woodpeckers known to store food and it is the only one known to cover the stored food with wood or bark. It make its own LARDER!




  • Omnivorous diet including a wide variety of insects, spiders, earthworks, nuts, seeds, berries, fruit, and occasionally small rodents. May sometimes eat eggs and nestlings of other birds
